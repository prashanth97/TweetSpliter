About Us

TweetSplitter allows you to write long tweets (more than 140 characters) and publish them in Twitter all together. You only have to write what you want, TweetSplitter will split it for you into small pieces of text and will twit it for you. Easy, isn't it?

TweetSplitter won't save information about tweets or users. We will just split the tweet and send it: easy for you, easy for us. Twitter has some limits for your tweets, depending on the user
